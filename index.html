Welcome to F1 stats

How to use

To use this page, please select a season from the list to show season details, or select a circuit to see more info about that round. The current year and round are underlined in white. Future races are blurred and highlighted in red.

Mobile

Use the down arrows on the top of the navigation to expand the seasons and round selections

Season results

To see a whole season, click on a year above. If it's the current year, you'll see the predicted reults.

The predicted results use a weighted calculation based on average finish and grid positions this season, plus finish and grid poaitions in the previoud two years at that circuit. You can click on each result to view the calculation steps.

Race results

To view an individual race result click on a track icon. To view other years click on the season followed by the circuit icon for that year.

Head 2 Head

When viewing a race result you can also compare two drivers. Use the A and B buttons to select drivers for compare. The Head2head section will show you comparisons between position, points, gains since start, fastest lap, top speed and a graph charting every lap time and position during the race.

Circuit info

When viewing a race the lower section will show you track sectors and a google map
